Overview
leek History Manager: Powerful search, bulk export, delete custom selections, filter by day/hour, and block domains from history.

BetterHistory makes managing your browsing history simple and efficient. Quickly find past visits with advanced search tools, including keywords and RegEx. Export history as CSV or HTML, block specific domains for privacy, and sync tabs across devices. Use the Date Selector to jump to any day or hour, and delete individual entries or full days with ease. Enjoy smooth navigation with light/dark modes and enhanced tools.

Stay in control with Smart Auto Cleanup, which automatically manages your browsing history by clearing it based on your preferred schedule and retention duration. Preserve important activity with Whitelist Domains, allowing you to exclude trusted sites from cleanup operations.

⭐ Features Detailed explanations

(1) Efficient History Search with Advanced Search Tools
Easily explore your browsing history using the intuitive Date Selector, which lets you jump to any specific date or hour with just a click. Your history is neatly organized by hours and days, making it simple to find the exact moment you need. Whether revisiting a webpage from last week or a few hours ago, BetterHistory ensures seamless access.

Additionally, locate specific details with ease using simple keywords or advanced Regular Expressions (RegEx). Whether it's a page title, URL, or visit timestamp, BetterHistory’s powerful search tools deliver precise and refined results effortlessly.

(2) Comprehensive History Deletion Options
Delete your browsing history with ease and precision:
• Remove history for a specific date or hour.
• Delete all visits to a specific domain or individual web pages.
• Filter history by keywords to remove only what’s necessary.

These options ensure you maintain control over your digital footprint with minimal effort.

(3) Smart Auto Cleanup
Keep your browser clean and your history private—automatically. With Smart Auto Cleanup, you can schedule regular cleanups and define how long your history should be retained. Whether it's clearing history daily, weekly, or after a custom duration, this feature ensures your browsing data is managed without manual effort.

(4) Whitelist Domains
Maintain important records while cleaning the rest. Whitelist Domains allows you to protect specific websites from being deleted during automated cleanups. It’s ideal for preserving access to frequently visited or trusted sites—ensuring your essential history stays intact while everything else is wiped.

(5) Export History in Multiple Formats
Export your browsing history with full flexibility to meet your needs:

• CSV Format: Ideal for creating spreadsheets, analyzing your history, or keeping detailed logs.
• HTML Format: Perfect for viewing or sharing your browsing history in a web-friendly format.

You can choose to export history for a complete day, a specific hour, or custom-selected history items, giving you total control over what gets exported.

(6) Domain Blacklist for Better Privacy
Block specific domains from being recorded in your history with the new Blacklist feature.

• Add or remove domains directly from the extension settings or instantly from the context menu while browsing.
• Protect your privacy by ensuring certain websites are never logged in your browser history again, making this feature perfect for sensitive browsing.

(7) Enhanced Context Menu Tools
Take advantage of the enhanced right-click options for quick actions:

• Search History: Instantly search your browsing history for selected keywords or a specific domain.
• Blacklist Domains: Quickly blacklist any domain without leaving the website you're on.
• Delete History: Remove specific pages, entire domains, or keyword-related history directly from the context menu.

This feature provides unmatched convenience, allowing you to manage your history without interrupting your browsing experience.

(8) Unified View of Browsing and Downloads
Access your browsing history and download logs in a single, unified interface.
This combined view eliminates the need to switch between multiple tabs or tools, saving time and enhancing productivity.

(9) Sync Across Devices for Seamless Browsing
View open tabs and manage browsing activity from all your synced devices, including desktops, mobile phones, and tablets.
BetterHistory ensures you never lose track of important tabs or browsing sessions, no matter which device you’re using.

(10) Dark Mode for a Better Experience
The user-friendly dark interface also enhances readability and minimizes glare, providing a more comfortable browsing experience.
